How to use LabyrinthVPN

To use LabyrinthVPN, tap the "Connect" button on the Home Screen. If the Android system prompts you for connection permission, tap "OK" or "Accept" to proceed.

How long can I stay connected with LabyrinthVPN?

For as long as you like! We don't impose any time or data limits on each connection.

Why can't I use the internet after the connection is established?

First, check your network connection. Next, ensure that you've disabled battery optimization for LabyrinthVPN in Android's battery settings (allowing background activity for LabyrinthVPN).

How do I disable battery optimization for LabyrinthVPN?
  • For most Android devices:
    1. Open Android's Settings.
    2. Navigate to Battery > Battery optimization.
    3. Select All apps from the dropdown menu.
    4. Find LabyrinthVPN and set it to Don't optimize.
  • For Huawei devices:
    1. Open Android's Settings.
    2. Navigate to Battery > Launch.
    3. Find in the app list.
    4. Switch to Manage manually.
    5. Enable Auto-launch and Run in background.
    6. Alternatively, check the Protected Apps setting for battery options.
  • For Samsung devices:
    1. Open Android's Settings.
    2. Navigate to Battery > App Power Saving > Details.
    3. Find LabyrinthVPN in the app list and disable power saving for it.
  • For other devices:
    1. Open Android's Settings.
    2. Navigate to Battery.
    3. Look for options such as Background activity or Protected apps.
